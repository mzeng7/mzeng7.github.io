Blog

I started this blog, entitled This World and Me, in 2013. I wanted a space where I could freely write about anything I wanted. No time limits, no due dates, no restrictions. I think there's something magical about an empty text box on a computer screen. It could turn into a story, a song, an argument, a letter, a computer program, a philosophical musing – the possibilities are limited only by the extent of one's creativity and imagination.
